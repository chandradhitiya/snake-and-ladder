How to Play

  1. Choose the number of players (2-4)
  2. Enter names for each player
  3. Click "Roll Dice" on your turn
  4. Roll a 6 to start playing
  5. If you land on a snake, you move down
  6. If you land on a ladder, you climb up
  7. First player to reach exactly 100 wins!
